Blood Alcohol Concentration and Its Effects

BAC Calculator (Blood Alcohol Content Calculator) is used to determine levels of drunkeness and know about its impairment on individuals.

The severity of impairments caused by Alcohol Intoxication level in bloodstream varies with BAC. On reaching 0.50% of BAC may lead to death.


BAC level and Its Impairments

  • 0.00% - 0.05%: Negligible Effects on people, with relaxed and slighter talkative nature.
  • 0.06% - 0.09%: Impaired Judgement and poor Coordination.
  • 0.10% - 0.14%: Dangerous behaviour with impaired Cognitive skills.
  • 0.15% - 0.19%: Motor skills severely affected that impacts Driving much.
  • 0.20% and above: Extremely impaired motor skills, affecting Driving skills.

Alcohol consumption may affect your heart and may lead to diabetes. It may cause Ischemic stroke and this may arise some problems like Mental confusion, Vomitting, slow heart rate and many others. Alcohol Intoxicatiion may sometimes lead to serious Complications. threats.

FAQs - Blood Alcohol Concentration Calculator

How is BAC calculated?

The BAC is calculated as gram of in 100 ml blood. Using the BAC Calculator you can better calculate BAC.


What is the ideal BAC?

It is appropriate to have 0.04 - 0.05% BAC.


What is the BAC for 6 beers in 4 hours?

It is estimated to be 11 if an individual weighs about 120 pounds and intake about 6 beers within 4 hours.


At what BAC do you get a hangover?

Drinking more than Normal drinking levels might lead to Hangover. It may happens when BAC reaches 0.11% of threshold value.


What BAC is blackout?

Blackouts occur when the blood alcohol concentration level reaches 0.16% or higher. Drunken's Cognitive abilities are severely affected at these Blood alcohol content levels.


What is your BAC after 1 beer?

BAC may range from 0.016 to 0.038 on consumption of 1 beer, it may differ a little bit based on your Body.


At what BAC would a coma occur?

You may start loosing consciousness at BAC higher than 0.30 levels.
